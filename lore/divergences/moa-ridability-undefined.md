# Moa Ridability Undefined

## Summary

This proposed divergence file reserves space for a possible moa transport divergence. It is intentionally undefined until the biological and ecological mechanism is specified.

## Metadata

- Layer: Fundamental divergence
- Primary topic: moa ridability divergence
- Topics: moa, ridability, transport, proposed divergence
- Status: Proposed / undefined

## What Might Change

A moa lineage may become more suitable for riding or transport roles, creating a possible analogue to horses in human societies.

This divergence is not yet defined.

## When

No canonical time label is assigned. This remains a proposed divergence until a biological mechanism and emergence period are specified.

## Capabilities It Might Enable

- A ridable moa lineage could transform transport, warfare, route systems, and regional integration.
- The divergence would need to explain anatomy, temperament, domestication pathway, load limits, and ecological costs.

## Systems That Might Depend On It

- Long-distance overland transport.
- Mounted or pack-animal military systems.
- Regional integration systems that require more transport capacity than cassowary walking, carrying, roads, or waterways can provide.

## Open Questions

- Does ridability require a fundamental divergence, selective domestication, equipment, or all three?
- Which region and time period would plausibly support moa transport systems?
- What limits prevent moa transport from becoming an unexplained universal solution?
