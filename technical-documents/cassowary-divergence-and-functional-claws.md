# Cassowary Divergence and Functional Claws

Status: Draft

## Summary

This technical document defines the primary divergence event of Cassowary World: cassowaries developing functional grasping claws on their vestigial wings approximately twelve million years ago.

This is the founding biological divergence that enables later anatomical, behavioral, and civilizational changes.

## Definition

The "functional claws" divergence is the earliest accepted counterfactual event in the setting.

It describes a mutation-and-selection pathway in which vestigial wing structures become increasingly useful for grasping, manipulation, and environmental interaction over evolutionary time.

This document does **not** assume fully human-like hands. It defines the starting point for a long evolutionary pathway toward improved manipulation.

## Mechanism / Process (Draft)

The working hypothesis for canon development is:

1. A mutation makes vestigial wing claws more usable for grasping or stabilizing behavior.
2. That usability creates survival or reproductive advantages in specific ecological contexts.
3. Selection preserves and amplifies manipulative utility over many generations.
4. Later anatomical and behavioral adaptations build on that lineage, eventually supporting tool use and more complex material culture.

This should be treated as a long-duration evolutionary chain, not a sudden transformation.

## Constraints and Limits

- This is the single major divergence event; no later "magic" exceptions are allowed by default
- All later capabilities must be traceable to intermediate forms
- Manipulation ability must remain compatible with cassowary body plan and locomotion
- Claims about advanced dexterity require supporting technical documents

## Implications for Civilization and Story

- Supports the possibility of gradual tool use and environmental modification
- Creates a plausible biological foundation for preservation technology, infrastructure, and administration
- Forces technical lore to explain transitions instead of skipping steps
- Anchors the tone as speculative evolution rather than fantasy

## Guiding Principles Used

- Principle 1: One Divergence, Then No Magic
- Principle 2: Biology Shapes Civilization
- Principle 6: Technology Evolves Gradually and Unevenly
