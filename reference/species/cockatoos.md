# Cockatoos

## Summary

This reference explains the real-world cockatoo traits likely to matter for future Cassowary World communication, partnership, and ownership lore.

## Metadata

- Primary topic: cockatoos
- Topics: cockatoos, parrots, communication, social learning, animal partnership

## Useful Baseline

- Cockatoos are parrots with strong social learning capacities.
- Cockatoos can be long-lived relative to many birds.
- Cockatoos are capable of vocal learning and complex social communication.
- Cockatoos can manipulate objects with beaks and feet.
- Cockatoo intelligence and sociality make them plausible partners for communication-oriented systems.

## Relevance to Cassowary World

Future cockatoo lore should build on real cockatoo cognition, sociality, and communication rather than treating cockatoos as arbitrary messenger animals.
